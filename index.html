WHAT IS CONCERTS IN TOWN?

Concerts In Town is a web-based tool in which artists can publish information about their upcoming concerts for fans to view.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

To begin, head over to the New Submissions tab to create an artist profile, you can include a profile
picture and a link to your spotify page! Always make sure to remember your password!

To submit a concert listing, click Concert Submission on the New Submissions tab to open the dropdown.
You will need your artist Username and Password to submit a listing! You can include a link to where tickets
for your concert can be purchased. Concert listings will automatically include your profile picture that you
added to your artist profile.

Once you've made a submission, they will appear at the bottom of the appropriate Artists or Concerts page!
